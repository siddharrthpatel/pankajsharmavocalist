The Artist

Dr. Pankaj Sharma: A Distinguished Classical Musician

Dr. Pankaj Sharma is a young and exceptionally talented artist of North Indian classical music. Born in Sujangarh, Rajasthan, he received his initial musical training under the guidance of eminent artists Sri Praveen Sharma, Sri Kamal Sharma, and the late Sri Shubhkaran Panwar of Sujangarh. Subsequently, he pursued formal training in classical music within the traditional guru-shishya (teacher-student) parampara under the tutelage of Dr. Ramgopal Tripathi of Varanasi.

After completing his secondary education in Rajasthan, Pankaj pursued higher studies at Banaras Hindu University (BHU), Varanasi. Under the guidance of distinguished faculty members from the Department of Music and Performing Arts at BHU, he successfully completed all levels of advanced classical music education. Currently, he continues his advanced training in classical music under the mentorship of Dr. Ramshankar, a principal disciple of the renowned Pandit Ramashraya Jha of the "Ramrang" tradition.

Professional Passion

Pankaj Sharma has delivered successful performances on numerous prestigious platforms across the country and has contributed his expertise as a music educator in several leading academic institutions. He is currently serving as a music teacher at Mahatma Gandhi Kashi Vidyapith, Varanasi.

Musical Excellence

He is widely recognized for his soulful performances and refined teaching methodology. Rooted deeply in Hindustani classical music, he has carved a distinctive path through years of dedicated practice, passion, and heartfelt artistic expression.

His musical repertoire encompasses Khayal singing, devotional music, and semi-classical forms, demonstrating both remarkable versatility and profound depth. With a voice that conveys clarity, emotional depth, and technical control, Pankaj's performances resonate with sincerity and spiritual grace, captivating audiences with his authentic interpretation of the classical tradition.

Lineage & Training

Dr. Sharma has received education from two esteemed music gurus of Varanasi, Dr. Ramgopal Tripathi and Dr. Ramshankar. Currently, he is actively engaged in advancing the North Indian classical music tradition through stage performances and training.

Guru Intro

Dr. Ramgopal Tripathi: A Confluence of Musical Devotion and Scholarly Excellence

Dr. Ramgopal Tripathi is an illustrious name in the realm of Hindustani Classical and Semi-Classical music—an artist who has established a profound harmony between traditional rendition and academic research. As a torchbearer of the invaluable heritage of the Gwalior Gharana, he has imbibed the intricate nuances of music at the feet of his Guru, Pt. Ravindranath Pandey.

Through this sacred Guru–Shishya Parampara, Dr. Tripathi’s life and work are spiritually and artistically linked to the luminous legacy of the great musical saint, Pandit Omkarnath Thakur Ji. His singing is not merely a performance of notes but a reflection of years of penance (Sadhna) and uncompromising shastric purity.

Academic Research & Scholarly Contribution

Dr. Tripathi did not confine music to the stage alone; he provided it with a scientific and educational perspective. His most significant academic contribution remains his pioneering research:

  • Doctoral Research on Mirjapuri Kajri: In 1997, Dr. Tripathi completed his Ph.D. from the Banaras Hindu University (BHU) on the distinctive folk-classical genre of "Mirjapuri Kajri.
  • Mentorship: He earned this prestigious doctorate under the expert and scholarly guidance of Prof. R. P. Shastri (Former Dean/HOD, Department of Vocal, Faculty of Performing Arts, BHU).
  • Legacy Preservation: By elevating this rare folk style to a classical academic platform, he has added a new chapter to the preservation of Indian musical heritage.

Professional Excellence & Global Mentorship

Dr. Tripathi’s contribution to the propagation and promotion of music has been multi-dimensional:

  • Service in KVS: His role as a Music Teacher within the Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan (KVS), under the Government of India, has been deeply impactful. He has mentored countless young talents, equipping them with the foundational knowledge of Indian music.
  • International Pedagogy: The light of his knowledge is not restricted to India; he has provided formal training in Hindustani Classical music to numerous students across the globe. His disciples now carry the banner of the Gwalior Gharana style in various corners of the world.
  • Academic Leadership: His persona represents a rare and ideal synthesis of traditional Guru-based training (Gurukul Parampara) and systematic academic research.

Dr. Ramshankar: A Global Luminary of Indian Classical Music & the Ramrang Tradition

Dr. Ramshankar is an internationally acclaimed vocalist and a premier exponent of Hindustani Classical Music. He received his rigorous musical training under the tutelage of the legendary scholar and composer, Pandit Ramashray Jha “Ramrang”.

He is widely recognized for his profound mastery of Raga Theory and his sophisticated command over complex vocal structures. As a dedicated practitioner and visionary scholar, Dr. Ramshankar has established a unique identity as the principal torchbearer of the "Ramrang Tradition." Currently, he serves as an Acharya (Professor) at the prestigious Banaras Hindu University (BHU).

Professional Excellence & Global Mentorship

Global Mission: The "Ramrang Sangeet Ramayan"

  • Global Institutionalization: He is profoundly committed to systematizing and performing the "Ramrang Sangeet Ramayan"—a masterpiece composed by Pt. Ramashray Jha—within the rigorous framework of Indian Classical Music.
  • International Recognition: Through his relentless efforts, he has established these compositions on international platforms, ensuring they are recognized as a vital part of the global classical repertoire.

Academic Research & Scholarly Excellence

Beyond his prowess on the stage, Dr. Ramshankar has significantly enriched the field of musicology through extensive academic contributions:

  • Published Works: He has authored numerous books, research chapters, and scholarly papers that serve as essential resources for students and researchers of Indian music worldwide.
  • Lectures & Workshops: He is a prominent figure in the global academic circuit, frequently conducting Lecture-Demonstrations (Lec-Dems), Workshops, and Seminars to propagate the nuances of Raga Vidya.
  • Global Mentorship: He has mentored a vast community of students across India and abroad, fostering a new generation of artists who uphold the purity of the Ramrang lineage.

Global Outreach & Cultural Propagation

Dr. Ramshankar’s musical journey transcends borders, positioning him as a global cultural ambassador of India:

  • International Concerts: Through his soulful and technically brilliant performances in various countries, he has played a pivotal role in the worldwide propagation of Hindustani Classical Music.
  • A Synthesis of Traditions: His work represents a rare and seamless integration of traditional Gurukul-based learning and modern university-level musical education.
